Notable Projects and Achievements

Final Year Research Project

I developed an alternative media consumption method that is based on combining video keyframes with subtitle text to create a comic-type experience. I found that the cognitive demands associated with watching subtitled material can often hinder the enjoyment of the content. Part of my project has been published at the ACM IMX’21 conference. I was also awarded the Sir James Black Award for this project for my outstanding contribution to research and scholarship in my field.
